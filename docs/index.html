Overview

The Oscars, sometimes referred to as the Academy Awards, are a renowned award given by the film industry to honor outstanding filmmaking.
Filmmakers, performers, and actresses have striven for the coveted Academy Awards, which honor exceptional accomplishments in a variety of categories, for more than nine decades.
These honors, which range from Best Picture to Best Director to Best Actor to Best Actress, have represented aesthetic excellence and acceptance within the filmmaking industry.
We set out on a fascinating journey through the history of the Academy Award-winning movies from 1930 to 2014 with our data visualization project.
We want to decipher fascinating trends and identify the patterns that have molded the Oscars and the film industry as a whole by analyzing this huge information.
We will examine the range of movies that have won this coveted award and their influence on the cinematic scene, from timeless classics to current favorites.
